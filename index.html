Katsu is a Japanese dish of crispy fried cutlets coated with Panko bread crumbs. Popular varieties are chicken katsu and tonkatsu (which is made with pork). Serve with white rice and tonkatsu sauce.

Mochi: Japanese Rice Cake

Mochi are small, sweet Japanese cakes made with glutinous rice flour (mochiko). They have a soft, chewy outer layer and a deliciously sticky filling made of sweetened red bean paste.
