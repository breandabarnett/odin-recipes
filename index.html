Onigiri: Rice Balls

This easy onigiri recipe is fun to make! These rice balls are a staple of Japanese lunchboxes. You can put almost anything in these rice balls; try salmon, pickled plums, and more!

Mochi: Rice Cake

Mochi are small, sweet Japanese cakes made with glutinous rice flour. They have a soft, chewy outer layer and a deliciously sticky filling made of sweetened red bean paste.
