Chicken Katsu (Japanese-style Fried Chicken)

Photo by Mitsuha Kirigaija


Description

Katsu is a Japanese dish of crispy fried cutlets coated with Panko bread crumbs. Popular varieties are chicken katsu (like this recipe) and tonkatsu (which is made with pork). Serve with white rice and tonkatsu sauce.
